Recycle Me!

In this physics based puzzle game, play as a little character on a rooftop in a city where waste falls from the sky. Your goal is simple: toss the garbage into their respective bins without falling off or allowing too much garbage to pile up! Planned for release September 2024.

SpellFister

Doom-style magic first-person shooter. Fight waves of enemies, level up and gain upgrades. Currently in "Beta" with a huge update and rerelease planned for late 2024 as a roguelite.
